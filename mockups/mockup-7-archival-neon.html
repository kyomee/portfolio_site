001   About

Brand & Narrative Architect

Brands don't fail because they lack creativity. They fail because, at some point, they lose clarity.

I work at the intersection of brand narrative and structure — helping organisations clarify who they are, so every decision that follows has a foundation to build on.

Across roles at Razer, KRAFTON, and Hill & Knowlton, I've seen the same root issue: misalignment. Teams generate constant activity and content, but without a coherent brand narrative to anchor them, it scatters.

As AI accelerates content production, the value shifts to human judgment — to defining meaning, understanding context, and knowing what a brand should and shouldn't say. That's the work I do.
