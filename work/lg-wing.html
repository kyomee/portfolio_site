Overview

Repositioned LG's swivel-screen smartphone as a creator tool through a 21-market influencer campaign. Rather than leading with technical specs, the strategy showed daily dual-screen value through authentic creator-led content.

Results
  • 12M+ campaign views; 4.5× engagement rate versus prior launch benchmarks.
  • 30% higher watch-through rate compared to traditional product launch videos.
  • Successfully repositioned LG WING as practical, creator-friendly, and aspirational.
Objective

Launch LG's swivel-screen smartphone by demonstrating genuine daily dual-screen value — moving away from spec-led marketing toward use-case storytelling.

Strategy — Feature → Benefit → Use Case
  • Built a use-case narrative framework: multitasking, creator tools, navigation, and entertainment.
  • Partnered with mega influencers across 21 markets to translate UX benefits in authentic, platform-native formats.
  • Shifted launch communications from spec announcements toward engagement-focused demonstrations.
  • Designed story angles per influencer type: recording/preview, navigation+media, creator mode, split-screen multitasking.
Execution
  • Delivered content across YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok with platform-specific formats for each market.
  • Negotiated and managed influencer partnerships, driving 30% gains in campaign efficiency.
  • Produced LG Mobile's first virtual product unveiling event, setting a new benchmark for future launches.
