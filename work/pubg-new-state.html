Overview

Led full-funnel content strategy for the global launch of PUBG: NEW STATE — a AAA next-generation mobile title entering 175+ countries simultaneously. The challenge was differentiating clearly from PUBG Mobile while managing a high-stakes global rollout.

Results
  • 50M+ global pre-registrations; strong creator and influencer amplification across regions.
  • Improved player comprehension of new mechanics via simplified, staged content.
  • Strengthened content consistency across development and marketing teams globally.
Objective

Lead the global launch of a AAA next-generation mobile title with full-funnel strategy, clearly differentiating PUBG: NEW STATE from PUBG Mobile for both existing fans and new audiences.

Strategy — Testing → Differentiation → Global Content
  • Alpha and beta test insights translated into transparent pre-launch content to build anticipation and trust.
  • Core differentiators (futuristic world, advanced movement, vehicle physics, improved combat) converted into clear user messaging.
  • Diversified content formats: cinematic trailers, teasers, music storytelling, in-game mechanic explainers.
  • Global influencer plan scaled across US, EU, and APAC regions with localized angles.
Execution — Pre → Launch → Post
  • Pre-launch: worldbuilding teasers, dev diaries, alpha/beta testing recaps to sustain community momentum.
  • Launch: global cinematic trailers and creator-led breakdown videos for key markets.
  • Post-launch: update explainers and patch-note breakdowns to maintain player engagement.
