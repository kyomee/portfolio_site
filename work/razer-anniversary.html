Overview

Built a unifying brand system for Razer's 20th anniversary — a milestone campaign spanning esports, lifestyle, and hardware across 15+ markets. The work reframed two decades of innovation into a forward-facing AI-guided narrative without losing the gamer-first identity Razer was built on.

Results
  • Consistent global brand narrative across 15+ markets.
  • Reinforced legacy while defining Razer AI as the next strategic chapter.
  • Improved production efficiency and cross-functional alignment across product, digital, and retail teams.
Objective

Strengthen Razer's global brand positioning for its 20th anniversary while defining the next AI-guided chapter and unifying narrative across markets.

Strategy — Brand Positioning → Legacy → Razer AI Next Chapter
  • Developed core positioning translating 20 years of legacy into an AI-powered evolution.
  • Positioned Razer AI as a continuation of the engineering, esports, and gamer-first heritage.
  • Built a cohesive global narrative: "20 years of innovation built together with our community."
  • Introduced brand governance and cross-market alignment frameworks for 15+ regions.
Execution
  • Full-year anniversary campaign with multi-format content across video, editorial, and experiential.
  • Video series, editorial artbook, offline store branding, and community activations.
  • Overarching Razer AI narrative across all streams; aligned product, digital, and retail teams.
  • Managed creative partners and studios across four continents to maintain premium quality at scale.
